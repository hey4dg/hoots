Event Formatting Rules

On our scheduling page, please keep your events in the following format:

Title: Course Codes
Who: Full Name | Comp ID | Cell # (or other method of contact) | Hourly Rate, Acceptable methods of payment
Where: Preferred Location

Once a mentee has booked you, go back into the schedule and type "BOOKED" before the course codes.

Go to the "scheduling" page using the navigation bar. Click to create your event. For booking purposes, it may be easier to create events that are only one hour each.



Example:
MATH 3255, 3351, 3310
Thomas Jefferson | taj4bc | 555-555-5555 | $20/hour, Venmo: @myvenmo, Cash (Probably best to Copy-Paste this line)
Clemons Library

Example:
BOOKED MATH 3255, 3351, 3310

*Note: our admins will delete events that don't follow this format.
